# SEO Local Intent Skill

Reusable checklist for improving WB Studio SEO around Saudi/GCC private villa and house design clients.

## Audience

- Target Saudi and GCC clients planning private houses, villas, family homes, and luxury residences.
- Write for decision-makers who value trust, premium taste, privacy, clear process, and regional understanding.

## Search Intent

- Align pages with terms such as architect for villa design, house design Saudi Arabia, Jeddah architect, luxury villa design, private home architect, and modern Saudi villa.
- Match intent naturally through page purpose, headings, metadata, service copy, image text, and calls to action.
- Avoid keyword stuffing; keep wording premium, minimal, and client-facing.

## SEO Checklist

- This skill may update itself when website changes, new assets, new media, new pages, SEO findings, or durable positioning decisions make an update valuable. Keep updates concise and high-impact. Do not update the skill for temporary ideas, minor wording preferences, low-value observations, private data, credentials, or noise.
- Title tags: make each page specific, benefit-led, and locally relevant where appropriate.
- Meta descriptions: summarize the offer clearly with Saudi/GCC relevance and a conversion-focused CTA.
- Arabic/English alignment: ensure keywords and meaning support both audiences without awkward repetition.
- H1/H2 structure: use one clear H1 and logical H2s for services, location relevance, process, portfolio, and contact intent.
- Internal anchors: link visitors toward key sections such as services, portfolio, process, location, and contact/WhatsApp.
- Image alt text: describe the project, room, style, and villa/house relevance without stuffing keywords.
- Schema.org basics: consider LocalBusiness, ProfessionalService, Organization, WebSite, BreadcrumbList, and relevant service schema when useful.
- Sitemap/robots: keep canonical URLs, sitemap entries, and robots directives consistent and crawlable.
- Open Graph previews: set page titles, descriptions, images, and URLs for premium sharing previews.

## Local And Client Intent

- Make location relevance explicit for Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, and GCC clients.
- Clarify remote design workflow when serving clients outside Jeddah or across the GCC.
- Use copy that signals premium residential expertise, family-home sensitivity, privacy, and practical project guidance.
- Prefer concise, elegant phrasing over dense SEO text.
